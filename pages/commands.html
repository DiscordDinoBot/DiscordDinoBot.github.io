Commands

/study

Allows you to start a Pomodoro session. You can choose between different levels of difficulty or pick custom lengths of time.

/stats

Showcases the amount of time the user has studied over the course of different time ranges. Data is recorded in Pacific Time (PT).

/timer

Allows you to begin a timer. Amount of time used by the timer will be added to the users statistics.

/help

Previews the commands for the bot and additional information.
